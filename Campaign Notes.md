## Key Locations
### The Afterlife Casino
A Nine Hells-themed attraction located just outside of town near the River Styx (referred to colloquially as "the river sticks"). It is built into a cavern system with a waterfall drop of approximately 100 feet.
* **The Docks:** Accessed via boat/ferry through underground channels. A red-robed tiefling named Jim greets visitors here.
* **The Main Floor:** Features slot machines, "Life and Death" card tables, and a central racing track for rats.
* **High Roller Area:** Contains premium Three Dragon Ante tables.
* **Tournament Area:** A sunken floor of black basalt pillars hosting the Three Dragon Ante tournament. The prize is a golden angel statuette holding a red gem.
* **Security Features:** The casino utilizes "security mirrors" that act as magical cameras, transmitting live images to a security office. 
* **Employee Areas:** Identified by bright **bright green trim** on doors; these areas are magically locked and require employee pass cards for access.
* **Quentin's Office:** A lavish office containing an L-shaped desk sized for a gnome, cushioned chairs, and a north wall relief depicting winged devils.
