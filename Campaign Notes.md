## Plot Developments
### Recent Events
* **The Security Confrontation:** While attempting to investigate cheating via Quentin Togglepocket, the party used a *Sleep* spell on security guards. This backfired when the verbal components alerted security, leading to an immediate combat encounter with casino guards.
* **The Jailbreak Attempt (Failed):** During a chaotic brawl involving magical sleep and unexpected reinforcements, Oink/Bonk and Shadowclaw were overpowered by security forces. They were stripped of all possessions and belongings, which were placed in padlocked chests. 
* **Elowen's Detainment & Escape:** Elowen was briefly detained by guards after being seen with the arrested party members but successfully used persuasion to avoid arrest and was permitted to visit the spa before being effectively banned from the casino floor for the night.
* **The Vault Discovery:** While scouting as a mouse, Elowen discovered a high-security vault containing an animated skeleton of a minotaur and multiple treasure chests. 

### Current Objectives
* **Escape/Reunion:** The primary goal is to reunite the incarcerated party members with Elowen and find a way to reclaim their stripped possessions.
* **Retrieve Assets:** Reclaim stolen equipment, specifically the "mystical bag" containing weapons and items.
* **Investigate the Vault:** Determine the identity of the necromancer controlling the skeletal minotaur in the vault.

## Key Locations
### The Afterlife Casino
A Nine Hells-themed attraction built into a cavern system.
* **The Docks:** Accessed via boat/ferry through underground channels. 
* **The Main Floor:** Features slot machines, "Life and Death" card tables, and a central racing track for rats.
* **Security Office:** Contains monitors linked to security mirrors. Currently under high alert following the recent skirmish.
* **The Jail Cells:** A series of six floor-to-ceiling barred cells located in the southern part of the casino. The party members are currently separated into individual cells.
* **The Restaurant:** Located approximately 20 feet below the casino floor, situated behind a waterfall. Features a dining area and a live violinist.
* **The Spa/Bath Area:** A humid, sulfurous room featuring steam pools, massage tables, and hot tubs.
* **The Circus Arena:** An area where animals are held; contains cages with baboons, acrobats, and a lion. Access to the animal holding area is via a door from the employee area.
* **The Vault:** A 40-foot deep chamber containing seven treasure chests, wardrobes, and an animated skeleton minotaur. It is heavily guarded by magical/mechanical means.
